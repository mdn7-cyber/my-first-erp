About OGI Campus:-

Oriental Group of Institutes, a premier engineering college, produces comprehensively trained, innovative engineers and enterprising, competent professionals.
Founded by Dr. K L Thakral in 1995, Oriental Group of Institutes imparts quality education in Engineering, Pharmacy, Management, and Computer Applications. The Courses are Affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal and Barkatullah University, Bhopal, Oriental Group of Institutes stands as one of the best Professional colleges in central India
The Institute has lush green campus and occupying more than 16 acres land on the State highway (76). The noise and pollution free environment acts as a stimulant for academic activities. The campus has planned buildings, laboratories, library, hostels and sports complex that catches the eye of every passerby.
